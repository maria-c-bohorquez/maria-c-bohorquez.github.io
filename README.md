<h2 style="font-size: 26px; font-weight: 700; color: #111111; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 20px;">
  Transforming the Nintendo eShop into an AI-Powered Discovery Hub
</h2>

<p style="color: #3f3f46; font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.6; max-width: 700px;">
  <b>The Challenge:</b> The Nintendo eShop functions as a flat, transactional checkout counter rather than a discovery hub, causing massive user browsing fatigue and completely burying high-margin indie titles.
</p>

* **Wishlist (Private & Transactional)** &mdash; *"I want to buy this title later."* Tracks immediate buying intent and checkout funnels.
* **Game Stash (Public & Curational)** &mdash; *"Look at what I have curated."* Acts as a digital trophy shelf to drive community validation and long-tail indie discovery.

<p style="color: #3f3f46; font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.6; max-width: 700px; margin-top: 15px;">
  <b>The Implementation Strategy:</b> Instead of incurring heavy engineering overhead, this roadmap maximizes the ROI of existing corporate infrastructure. By expanding Nintendo's Switch 2 AI discovery model to ingest cross-platform data endpoints, the strategy targets a <b>15% reduction in search bounce rates</b>, turning passive browsing into high-margin digital checkouts.
</p>

<h2 style="font-size: 26px; font-weight: 700; color: #111111; margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 20px;">
  HeartTunes: VR Rhythm System for Immersive Adolescent Regulation
</h2>

<p style="color: #3f3f46; font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.6; max-width: 700px;">
  <b>The Challenge:</b> Traditional adolescent socioemotional training modules suffer from high user disengagement. This project designed a native Unity prototype on Oculus Quest that transforms rhythm mechanics and tactile audio loops into explicit tools for user emotional awareness and regulation.
</p>

* **Cross-Functional Co-Design:** Facilitated design thinking workshops with educators at <i>Coschool Colombia</i> in Cali to align game loops directly with active classroom learning methodologies.
* **Technical Ownership:** Orchestrated end-to-end user journey mapping, software QA validation, and audio track engineering with sound designers to ensure precise interaction tracking.
* **Industry Stature:** Project presented to Toronto Metropolitan University in fulfillment of a Master of Digital Media and formally selected for presentation at the international **IEEE GEM 2022** conference.
